# Design Rules

## Fonts

Only these two fonts may be used:

- Baskerville for accents, display moments, elegant headings, quotes, and decorative emphasis.
- San Francisco for everything else, including body text, buttons, labels, inputs, navigation, tables, and UI text.

No other fonts may be used.
